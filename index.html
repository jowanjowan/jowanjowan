Core Value

Praise and Worship

bible verse

Praise and worship is vital in every believer to access God.Worship meets Gods needs because God is seeking for worshippers who will worship Him  in Spirit and truth.

praise and worship(john 4:23) ushers us into Gods presence and we know that He dwells in the praise and worship of His people.

Leader and choir  is to help the worshipper in to congregation express themselves in worship to the lord,giving sacrifice of their lips.

Its the expression of the worshippers love to the LOR, in practice,the practice of God ministers to the worshippers with benefits which include joy,deliverance and healing, therefore the worship part of any service is very crucial and very important to the Shiloh Worship Centre Community.    

 

Prayers

bible verse

Prayer is communication with the Father an d very vital element in a believers walk with God.Shiloh Worship Centre is founded in prayer.Its our backborne.We belive that God is great and awesome and that specific prayers,request and action brings change to both individual lives and corporately,expotential power is released when we get together for prayers.

Its the way only through which we make our supplication and request known to the Father. 

The Word

bible verse

The teaching and preaching  of the word  is the hallmark of Shiloh Worship Centre.

We believe that its is the knowledge of truth that brings freedom.

"You shall know the truth  and the truth shall make you free" 

We uphold the teachiing and the preaching of the word of God paramountly so that people may get rooted in the truth for the purposes of practical living.

Releasing Believers

bible verse

 

 

Every believer is a minister gifted in one way or another.The purpose of the gifting is to Glorify God and edify the body of Christ.In Shiloh Worship Centre,we believe in staring up the gift in every believer and releasing them to ministry by creating an enabling environment for  believers to practice  their gifts to benefit the body of Christ and the extention of Gods kingdom. 

 

 

 

Ministries

bible verse

The main reason for our existence here at Shiloh Worship Centre is to reconcile people to God and to people.

One of the core values is to release  the believer to serve in the ministr having been equipped  and rooted firmly in the word of God.

Ministry is important to us because we believe that every believer forms an integral part of the body of Christ "For the body is not one member but many".....1st Corinthians 12:14

Their unique gifts and abilities are meant to glolify , edify the body of Christ and extend the Kingdom of God...

Growth comes from building on those special abilities,strength,gifts or talents in an individual.

The more they are exploited,the more they provide insight, revelation and information which cannot be received through any other mean resulting to growth.

The ministries at Shiloh Worship Centre are designed to provide for an opportunity for each member of the family to exploit  their gifting in the area where they feel they have grace to serve.

 They are designed to creat an enabling environment where believers can practice and exploit their gifting leading to growth of the Kingdom of GOD...

"As ye have done it unto one of the least of those my brethren, that ye have done it unto me" Mathew 25:40

About Us

Shiloh Worship Centre
Mwariki

Shiloh worship Centre-Mwariki is a fast growing vibrant family church.We work with the community reaching out beyond our congregation to those in our neighbourhood, to demonstrate the love of God

We are multi racial church where: 

Everybody is somebody and Christ is the head

Our objective is to meet the needs of every human being  regardless of colour,creed or beliefs.We believe in raising people  to fulfil their dreams and doing so we have beeen commissioned by God to help people become real worshippers.

At Shiloh , we offer  range of ministries and services for different age group designed to help each member in their daily  walk with Christ.
